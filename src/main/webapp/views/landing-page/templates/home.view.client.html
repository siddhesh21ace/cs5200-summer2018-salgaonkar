About Poképedia

What is Poképedia ?

Poképedia is my attempt to emulate the hugely popular Pokémon Universe on the web. The plan includes creation of the game cum website which simulates all things Pokémon like Pokédex module which emulates the Pokédex (a detailed listings for Pokémon) from the game, Gym battles and a leaderboard for the users of the system.

Pokémon Trading Card Game (TCG)

Play the Game!

Pokémon Trading Card Game is my attempt to create a web version of the hugely popular Pokémon Trading Card Game.

The basic game play remains similar to the original game. Players assume the role of a Pokémon trainer and use their Pokémon to battle their opponents.

A Pokémon gets knocked off if it's HP falls below 0.

The player wins when the opponents Pokémon is knocked off. A pokemon is gifted to the winner and added to his profile.

What is Pokédex?

Pokédex The Pokédex is an electronic device designed to catalogue and provide information regarding the various species of Pokémon featured in the Pokémon video game, anime and manga series.

The name Pokédex is a neologism including "Pokémon" (which itself is a portmanteau of "pocket" and "monster") and "index". The Japanese name is simply "Pokémon Encyclopedia", and it can feature every Pokémon on it, depending on the Pokédex.

Every Pokémon trainer has a Pokédex for identifying Pokémons they come across in their quest to catch 'em all.

Rules of the Game

The screen is divided into two parts:

The top half represents Gym Leader's Pokemons and its attacks. The bottom half of the screen belongs to you and your pokemons.

  • Attack! - Select the best possible attack you can
  • Wait! - Wait for "Gym Leader" to finish its attack
  • HP Matters! - Keep an eye on your Pokemon's HP (Hit Points)
